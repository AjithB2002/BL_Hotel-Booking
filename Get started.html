New York

The hotel was new. I didn't like that our room was at least a quarter mile from the elevator, but we lived. ... Thank you for your stay with us. ... New York theme was on point.The ultra-modern Thompson Central Park – New York provides tempting restaurants, an underground gym and a Drybar salon in an impressive urban location.

Located on Manhattan’s Billionaires’ Row, the hotel is steps from Central Park and Fifth Avenue. It also sits on the same block as Carnegie Hall, less than 500 metres from The Museum of Modern Art and approximately half a mile from Times Square. John F. Kennedy International Airport is 15 miles away.br

The sleek, modern rooms have a mid-century feel with walnut-style wood furniture accented by brass hardware. Subtle green, mustard and teal furnishings add contrast to the moody neutrals. Choose from King or 2 Double Rooms, and Studio Suites with larger seating areas. All rooms include blackout curtains to block out the bright city lights and D.S. & Durga toiletries – a high-end perfumery brand from Brooklyn. Opt for rooms located on higher floors for the best views.
